USAGE:
1. Select a book from the bookshelf. If the title is hard to see, you can hover on it to see the full title.
2. Once the book is opened, on the right you have set of words that each belongs to a group of eight parts of speech.
These represent most used word of that group. Words are color-coded and matches with the bookmark tags of eight parts of speech.
3. You can click on each bookmark-tag on the right to fetch a bar-graph. This bar-graph shows top 10 most used words that belongs to that part of speech.
